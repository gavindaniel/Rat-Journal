Project: Right Brain

My name is Gavin Daniel, and I grew up in a black hole.


That black hole, is called Wenatchee, Washington.

Ever since I was a kid, I had one of the biggest imaginations.
I could waste away hours, days, weeks, in my own bedroom.
Once I closed my door, I was in my own world.
What I soon realized was, I wanted to share that world with more people than just myself.
For years, I have tried to carry that same dedication and commitment to my imaginary world over to the real world.
I day-dreamed of imaginary worlds where my friends and I's lives revolved around what we loved at the time:
Legos,
Beanie Babies,
Building Forts,
Video Games,
Anything.
Yet, I never understood why no one ever shared the same interest, or maintained the same level of interest.
Sooner or later, people discovered new interests, new games would be released,
and all that progress would feel like for nothing...


It wasn't until May of 2017, when talking with my good friend Kevin...
that I finally figured it out.
Kevin has been the only person in my 21 years
that I have found to have a similar level of imagination, and dedication to the things he loves (video games).

Kevin and I were talking about the very few things to do in our beloved hometown.
We were listing off things to do when we got to Dragon's Lair at Eastmont Lanes.
My first idea to Kevin was what if there was a futuristic Arcade...
Where kids (even still at heart like Kevin and I)
could go and play ALL the games from today back to Pong.
That's when the lightbulb went off in my head...

What if...
There was a place where Kids could let there imaginations run wild...
Where the walls were your canvas...
and two dozens TVs lined the walls ready to have Nintendo 64s and Xbox Ones plugged in and played.
A place where kids begged their parents to let them stay "just one more hour" because they're having too much fun right now.
A place where parents regain the tranquility of their homes, and re-learn the meaning to un-wind after a long days work.
A place where the workers are more excited to come everyday than the Kids.
A place where time is mearly a measurement how much fun you're having.



We're not a Day Care.
Or a place to drop your trouble-some kids off of and hope they get tired out.

We're not a School.
And we're not going to force your kid to do anything they don't want to (other than follow our rules of safety).



We're a place to let your kid discover the Right-side of their Brain.
A place to show them how cool learning is.
Experience new things, and connect the dots.


All by their own choice.
